## **GHL Client Import**

<Warning>
  Ensure the client is a **sub-account** in your GHL Panel before importing.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a New Client via GHL">
    - Log in to your **Voicemetrics Agency Panel**.
    - Go to **Clients → Add New Client**.
    - Click **GHL Client Import**.

    ![Ghl2 Pn](/images/ghl2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="GHL Permissions">
    - Install **GoHighLevel (GHL)** from the Marketplace.
    - Enable **“Voicemetrics Call”** and click **Proceed**.
    - Select your client (must be listed as a **Sub-account** in GHL).

    ![Ghl1 Pn](/images/ghl1.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **GHL Settings**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to GHL">
    - Log in to your **Voicemetrics Agency account**.
    - Go to **Client Accounts → Open desired Client → GHL Settings**.
    - Click **Connect to GHL**.

    ![Ghl5 Pn](/images/ghl5.png)

    - Install **GHL** from the Marketplace.

    ![Screenshot(98) Pn](/images/Screenshot\(98\).png)

    - Enable **“Voicemetrics Call”** and click **Proceed**.
    - Select your client listed as a **Sub-account** in GHL.

    ![Ghl1 Pn](/images/ghl1.png)

    <Note>
      Keep both **Voicemetrics** and **GHL** open in two tabs of the same browser for a quick setup.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom Menu Link">
    - In the **Voicemetrics Agency account**, go to **Client Accounts → GHL Settings**.
    - Copy the **Custom Menu Link URL**

    <Tip>
      For example: https://ai.voiceagent.ai/#/embed/&locationid={{location.id}}&allow=&u={{user.name}}&c={{user.email}}
    </Tip>
    ![Ghl6 Pn](/images/ghl6.png)

    - In **GHL**, go to **Settings → Custom Menu Links**.
      - Click **Create New** and:
      - Choose an icon and name.
      - Paste the copied URL.
      - Enable **“Allow Microphone”**.
      - Select all sub-accounts.

    <Note>
      **All GHL CRM users can now access Voicemetrics inside GHL.**
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync Call Logs & Contacts">
    In **Voicemetrics → GHL Settings**, enable:

    - Auto-Create New Contact in GHL CRM
    - Auto-Sync Call History to GHL CRM
    - Log Call Summary as Notes
    - Auto-Sync GHL Contacts

    You can also:

    <Check>
      Set **Sync Frequency** (e.g., every 1 hour)
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Field Mapping">
    <Warning>
      Mapping options appear only if at least one contact exists in GHL CRM.
    </Warning>
    - Click **Add Field** to map additional contact fields.
    - Ensure accurate field mapping for precise data sync between Voicemetrics and GHL.

    ![Ghl9 Pn](/images/ghl9.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **FAQs**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to log in with the main GHL account or sub-account?">
    Log in with your **sub-account**. You’ll see the Voicemetrics menu inside.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use the custom menu link for multiple client accounts?">
    Yes. A one-time setup of the custom menu link works for all clients in GHL.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often does the contact sync run?">
    You can set your preferred sync frequency (e.g., every 1 hour).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if there are no contacts in GHL CRM?">
    Field mapping appears only after at least one contact exists in GHL.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I ensure call logs sync correctly?">
    Turn on **Auto-Sync Call History** and **Log Call Summary as Notes**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
