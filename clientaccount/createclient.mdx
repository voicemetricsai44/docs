## **Client Onboarding**

Get started quickly with two options: add clients manually or import from GHL.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Manual Client Setup" icon="snowman-head" color="#034737">
    - Add client from Agency Panel
    - Customize plans and settings
    - Assign licenses instantly
  </Card>
  <Card title="GHL Client Import" icon="file-import" color="#034737">
    - Import clients from GHL sub-accounts
    - Sync all client details automatically
    - Ensure the client exists as a sub-account
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## **Manual Client Setup**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Agency Panel" stepNumber={1} titleSize="p">
    - Navigate to the **Clients** menu and click **Add New Client**. 
    - Now, tap the **Manual Client Setup**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Account">
    - Enter the Client’s name and **Create Account.**
    - Your Client Account will now be ready.

    ![Manualclient Pn](/images/manualclient.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **GHL Client Import**

<Warning>
  Ensure the client is a **sub-account** in your GHL Panel before importing.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Agency Panel">
    - Navigate to the **Client Account** menu and click **Add New Client**. 
    - Now, tap the **GHL Client Import**

    ![Ghl2 Pn](/images/ghl2.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="GHL Permissions">
    - **Install GHL** from the Marketplace
    - Select your Client, which must be listed as **“Sub-account”** in GHL.
    - Enable “**Voicemetrics Call**” and click **Proceed.**

    ![Ghl1 Pn](/images/ghl1.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Enable Re-billing**

After the above steps, enable re-billing and set up your trial details.

<Note>
  Learn more about [Enable Re-Billing](https://docs.voicemetrics.ai/Rebilling/enablerebilling)
</Note>

Your client’s **trial account is now active and ready to use.**

![Rebill1 Pn](/images/rebill1.png)
