## **Assign SaaS Plans to a Client Account**

Easily assign customized SaaS plans to your client accounts. This streamlines your client onboarding by assigning tailored SaaS subscription plans to individual accounts.

## **Pre-requisite**

- Ensure your subscription tiers are set up beforehand
- Want to create one? [Link] Learn how to create SaaS Plans.

## **Steps for Agencies to Assign a SaaS Plan**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Agency Panel" stepNumber={1} titleSize="p">
    - Navigate to the "**Clients**" menu.
    - Click on the desired client name and open the "**Pricing**" tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Subscription" stepNumber={2} titleSize="p">
    - **Tick** the checkbox to assign a subscription plan.
    - The **first **selected plan will appear **last **in the client account's subscription list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Steps for Clients to Access SaaS Plans**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Client App" stepNumber={1} titleSize="p">
    - Navigate to the **Plan & Billing** Menu
    - View **available** subscription plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe to a Plan" stepNumber={2} titleSize="p">
    - Click "**Subscribe**" on your desired plan.
    - Review subscription details before confirming.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Upon successful subscription:**

  - A **Success** page will be displayed.
  - Clients will receive a **confirmation email** with the payment invoice.
  - Agencies will receive a **notification via email** to the billing contact.
</Note>
