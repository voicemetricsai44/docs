## **Set Up Your Subscription TiersÂ **

1. Log in to your **Agency Account**.
2. Navigate to **SaaS Setup** and select **Subscription Tiers**.
3. Click the **Create Subscription Tier** button to start setting up a new plan.

## **Design Your Pricing Plans**

**Core Details**

<Expandable title="Plan Name">
  Assign a unique name (e.g., "**Growth**").
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Plan Description">
  Briefly describe the plan's \*\*purpose \*\*(e.g., "Best for growing businesses").
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Monthly Fee">
  Set the subscription cost (e.g., **\$699**)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Annual Discount">
  Add \*\*savings \*\*for yearly payments (e.g., 10%).
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Free Minutes">
  Include a set number of minutes (e.g., **1,000** minutes).
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Extra Minute Charges">
  Specify rates for **additional usage** (e.g., \$0.29/min).
</Expandable>

**Enhancements**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Highlight as Most Popular:">
    Emphasize standout plans for better visibility.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Plan Features">
    Showcase up to 10 key benefits or highlights of the plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

After finalizing the details, click **Submit** to launch your pricing plan. This step makes your subscription tiers available to customers, helping you drive revenue and showcase your value proposition effectively.
