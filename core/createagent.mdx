## **Create a Client Account**

<Warning>
  Before creating an agent, set up a client account from your Agency Panel.
</Warning>

**Two ways to access the client account**

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Switch to Client Account" icon="light-switch" color="#034737">
    Choose the dropdown of your desired client account from the top left.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Access Account" icon="arrow-right-to-city" color="#034737">
    - Open the desired client account and tap "Access Account” in the top right corner
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Make sure to enable **Agency View** in the footer of the client account menu for full access.
</Tip>

## **Create a New Agent**

**Steps:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Inside the Client Account" stepNumber={1} titleSize="p">
    - Go to the **“Agents”** tab
    - Click **“Create an Agent.”**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose from Agent Library" stepNumber={2} titleSize="p">
    - **Option 1:** Use a **predefined template** from the library. (e.g., Appointment Booking).
    - **Option 2:** Start from a **blank template** to build your custom agent.
  </Step>
</Steps>
