## **Overview**

The **Brand Information** section allows agencies to completely personalize the platform, ensuring your clients interact with a solution that looks and feels like your own product.

You can customize your **logo**, **colors**, **contact details**, **support links**, and **billing information** — all under your agency’s identity.

## **What You Can Customize**

- Business Name & Website
- Brand Logos (Dark & Light Theme)
- Favicon
- Theme Colors (Primary & Secondary)
- Primary Contact Information
- Billing Information
- Support Links (up to 4 links)
