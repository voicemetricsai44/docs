## Comprehensive Call Insights at a Glance

Effortlessly access, review, and analyze all call interactions in one streamlined platform for seamless tracking and insights.

- **Summary**: Quick snapshot of key call details like agent, phone number, and duration.
- **Analysis**: Review call sentiment, goal status, and customer feedback.
- **Transcript**: Get a detailed conversation log for deeper insights.
- **Call Information**: Includes start/end times, duration, and reason for call termination.
