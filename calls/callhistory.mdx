## Comprehensive Call Insights at a Glance

Effortlessly access, review, and analyze all call interactions in one streamlined platform for seamless tracking and insights.

- **Overview**: Shows start/end times, duration, and termination reason.
- **Summary:** Quick snapshot of key call discussions.
- **Analysis:** Extract contact info like name, phone, and email.
- **Transcript:** Detailed conversation log for deeper insights.
