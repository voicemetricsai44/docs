## **Filters**

- Toggle between demo and live data.
- Filter by call status, goal status, call type, or agent.

## **Visual Insights**

- **Call Volume**: Trends in call connections.
- **Goal Conversion**: Success rates of calls.
- **Timing Analysis**: Peak call times.
- **Heat Map**: Visualize activity across timeframes.
- **A/B Testing**: Compare performance metrics.
- **Call Details**: Dive deeper into individual calls.

Effortlessly track and optimize your call strategies\!
