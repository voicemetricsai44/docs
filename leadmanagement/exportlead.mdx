---
title: "Export Leads"
description: "Export leads effortlessly into a CSV file for easy management and sharing."
---

## **Steps:**

1. Navigate to the Leads section and click **Import/Export** (top-right corner).
2. Select the desired lead list and click **Export CSV**.
3. **Verify** the number of contacts, then confirm the export.
4. **Please save** the file to your desktop and review it as needed.

Quick and seamless lead management at your fingertips\!
