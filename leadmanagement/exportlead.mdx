---
title: "Export Contacts"
description: "Export contacts effortlessly into a CSV file for easy management and sharing."
---

## **Steps:**

1. Navigate to the Contacts section and click **Import/Export** (top-right corner).
2. Select the desired contact list and click **Export CSV**.
3. **Confirm** to export or cancel to go back.
4. **Please save** the file to your desktop and review it as needed.

Quick and seamless contact management at your fingertips!
