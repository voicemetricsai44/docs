### **1. Manual Add**

1. Log in as a **Client**.
2. Click **Contacts \> Add Contact**.
3. Enter **Name** and **Phone Number** (e.g., John Doe, +14158516395).
4. Click “**Add,**” and it will save.

![C1 Pn](/images/c1.png)

### **2. Bulk Import**

1. Go to Contacts \> tap **(⋮) right top\> Import/Export**  (Right-side Top).
2. Upload a **CSV File** with columns like Name and Phone.
3. Match fields and click **Import**.

   <Note>
     Ensure phone numbers use the format +Country Code (e.g., +14158516395).
   </Note>

### **3. Webhook Integration**

1. Create a Webhook under **Contacts \> Webhooks** (Right-side Top).
2. Copy the webhook URL and add it to your website or ad platform.
3. Map fields to sync data in real-time.
   - **Example:** Use for auto-capturing leads from Google Ads.

     <Note>
       Use **View Logs** to troubleshoot and verify incoming lead data.
     </Note>

## **How to Update a Contact**

### **One-by-One Update**

1. **Open the Lead**: Click on the Contact Name (e.g., _John Doe_) to **access the contact.**
2. **Update Fields**: Edit fields like email, phone number, or status 
   - **Example**: Change status from **_New_ **to **_Qualified_**.

### **Mass Update**

1. **Select Contacts**: **Enable the checkboxes** next to multiple contacts
2. **Apply Updates**: Click the **Update** button (top-right corner).
3. **Choose Fields**: **Add or modify**  details like tags or move contacts to a specific list 
   - **Example**: Change tags from \_Followup \_to _Interested_
   - **Example**: Move leads to a specific list as \_Normal Clients _to VIP Clients._

<Note>
  Use descriptive **tags** and **statuses** for easier filtering and improved lead tracking.
</Note>

## **How to Filter or Search a Contact**

### **Types of Filters**

- **Lists**: Organize contacts into categories (e.g., _Premium Clients_, _New Prospects_).
- **Tags**: Use labels like _Interested_, _Follow-Up Needed_.
- **Status**: Filter by lead status (e.g., _Qualified_, _Pending_).

### **Steps to Filter or Search**

1. **Open the Dropdown**: Navigate to the contacts section and access the filter dropdown.
2. **Select Filter Type**: Choose Lists, Tags, or Status to refine your search.
   - **Example**: Select the _New Prospects_ list or the _Interested_ tag.
3. **View Results**: Leads matching your criteria will be displayed.

<Note>
  **Combine filters** (e.g., _Premium Clients_ + _Interested_) for precise results.
</Note>
