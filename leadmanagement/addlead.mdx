---
title: "Add Lead"
---

## **1. Manual Add**

1. Log in as a Client.
2. Click **Leads \> Add Lead**.
3. Enter **Name** and **Phone Number** (e.g., John Doe, \+14158516395).
4. Click “**Add Lead,**” and it will save.

## **2. Bulk Import**

1. Go to \*\*Leads \*\*\> **Import/Export** (Right-side Top).
2. Upload a **CSV File** with columns like Name and Phone.
3. Match fields and click **Import**.

   <Note>
     Ensure phone numbers use the format \*\*\+Country Code \*\*(e.g., \+14158516395).
   </Note>

## **3. Webhook Integration**

1. Create a Webhook under **Leads \> Webhooks** (Right-side Top).
2. Copy the webhook URL and add it to your website or ad platform.
3. Map fields to sync data in real-time.
   - **Example:** Use for auto-capturing leads from Google Ads.

     <Note>
       Use **View Logs** to troubleshoot and verify incoming lead data.
     </Note>
