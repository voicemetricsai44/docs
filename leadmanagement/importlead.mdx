## **How to Import Leads**

Importing leads simplifies customer data integration and enables efficient management. Use a CSV file to seamlessly upload contacts, organize them with lists and tags, and streamline communication for better results.

**Before You Begin**\
 Ensure your CSV file meets the following criteria

<Warning>
  Phone numbers must include a **country code with a plus sign** (\+), without spaces or dashes (e.g., \+14158516395).
</Warning>

## **Steps to Import Leads**

1. Go to the **Leads** section.
2. Click the **Import/Export**  button in the top-right corner (tap three dots).
3. Select **Choose a CSV File** and upload your file.
4. Ensure your CSV file meets the following criteria: phone numbers must include a country code with a plus sign (\+), without spaces or dashes (e.g., \+14158516395).
5. Choose or create a **list** and **tag** for the leads.
6. **Match CSV columns** (e.g., Name, Phone) to system fields.
7. Click **Import** to upload leads.
8. A **success** message will confirm the import.
