---
title: "Update Lead"
---

**One-by-One Update**

1. **Open the Lead**: Click on the Contact Name (e.g., _John Doe_) to **access the lead.**
2. **Update Fields**: \*\*Edit fields \*\*like email, phone number, or status 
   1. Example: Change status from _New_ to _Qualified_.

**Mass Update**

1. **Select Leads**: **Enable the checkboxes** next to multiple leads 
2. **Apply Updates**: Click the **Update** button (top-right corner).
3. **Choose Fields**:**Add or modify**  details like tags or move leads to a specific list 
   1. Example: Change tags from \_Followup \_to _Interested_
   2. Example: Move leads to a specific list as \_Normal Clients _to_VIP Clients._

**Note**: Use descriptive tags and statuses for easier filtering and improved lead tracking.
